About Me
----------

This is the digital media portfolio for
Madeline Zaytsoff.

Madeline is a student at Concordia University in Montreal who is currently working towards her bachelor’s degree in computational arts. Her focus lies primarily in the field of game development with specific attention given to technical artistry, environmental design, and in-house tool development. Though the projects she’s undertaken have varied in themes, a love of horror games is the driving creative factor in her work.

Currently, Madeline is working with a team to produce a 3D, first-person horror/puzzle game titled 'My Friend Aki'. Her role in this project primarily focuses on technical artisty and environmental design as well as art direction. A demo should be ready by October of 2023.
